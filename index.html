Privacy Policy for PawHab Thank you for using PawHab (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the App. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App. 1. Summary - We process your pet photos to analyze behavior and provide guidance. - Photos are sent securely to Google’s Generative AI services (Gemini API via Google AI Studio) for analysis. - We do not sell your data. - We do not use your images to train AI models; they are used only to generate results. - You can delete locally stored data at any time by clearing the App’s cache/storage. 2. Data We Collect 2.1 Photos and Media You Provide - When you choose an image from your gallery or capture one with your camera, the App temporarily accesses that image to perform analysis. - Images may be transmitted to a third-party AI service (Google Gemini) exclusively to generate the analysis results. 2.2 Device and Usage Data (App-internal) - Basic app diagnostics and error information may be collected locally to ensure functionality (e.g., crash or error messages). This is not linked to your identity. - This data is not transmitted externally and remains only on your device. 2.3 No Account or Personal Identifiers - The App does not require account creation or collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email, or phone number by default. 3. How We Use Your Data - To analyze the selected photo and generate behavior insights for dogs, cats, birds, or fish. - To improve functionality, performance, and user experience (e.g., troubleshooting issues). 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure - We share the selected image with Google’s Generative Language (Gemini) API strictly for the purpose of generating the analysis result. - We do not sell your data. - We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights. 5. Data Retention - Photos are processed on demand.
